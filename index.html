AKIRA
  • About
    • Akira (Japanese: アキラ) is a 1988 Japanese animated cyberpunk action film directed by Katsuhiro Otomo, produced by Ryōhei Suzuki and Shunzō Katō, and written by Otomo and Izo Hashimoto, based on Otomo's 1982 manga of the same name. Set in a dystopian 2019, it tells the story of Shōtarō Kaneda, a leader of a biker gang whose childhood friend, Tetsuo Shima, acquires incredible telekinetic abilities after a motorcycle accident, eventually threatening an entire military complex amid chaos and rebellion in the sprawling futuristic metropolis of Neo-Tokyo.

神秘的な爆発が都市を破壊してから20年以上後、黙示録的で未来的な「ネオトキョ」を舞台にしています。カネダの精神的に不均衡な幼少期の友人である清水を防止しようとする軍事指導者大佐は、彼の不安定で破壊的なテレキネティック能力を使用して都市を破壊し、「アキラ」という名前の強力な精神的能力を持つ神秘的な存在を目覚めさせます。 Otomoは、サイバーパンクのジャンルの慣習を使用して、政治的混乱、社会的孤立、腐敗、権力の物語を詳述しています。サイバーパンクの画期的な作品と広く見なされ、日本のサイバーパンクのサブジャンルの先駆者であると認められたアキラは、読者や批評家から普遍的な称賛を受けました。

The begining

As a nod to Neo-Tokyo, the main title was created as a neon sign. We researched and based off a Katakana type that gives the right vibe to our reinterpretation, with the rest of the artwork scattered with typographic references to the anime.

CHARACTER

Shotaro Kaneda

Kaneda stands at near average height with short black hair and dark brown eyes. His most notable feature is his attire in the film: a jacket with large design of a capsule on the back

Kaori

was Tetsuo Shima's love interest. Her role slightly differs from the manga, in that she appeared to have been in a relationship with Tetsuo prior to the events of the movie instead of meeting him as a slave.

Tetsuo Shima

was a member of a teenage biker gang and the main protagonist of Akira. After a near-collision with Takashi and being experimented by the Japanese military government, he rapidly develops powerful.

Kei

is one of the main characters of the Akira manga and anime franchise.In both she is a member of the Resistance.
